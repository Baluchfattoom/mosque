Our Materials

Our mosque is being crafted with meticulous attention to detail, using a variety of high-quality materials. Every element is chosen to create a serene and inspiring mosque. Wood, metal, concrete, and sturdy stones are also skillfully incorporated, adding functionality to our mosque designs. We take pride in the thoughtful selection of materials that reflect both tradition and innovation in our architectural creations.

REINFORCEMENT STEEL

Reinforcing concrete structures and providing additional strength to the mosque's framework, particularly in areas where tensile strength is required to withstand forces like tension and bending.

CONSTRUCTION SAND

It is specially processed and graded to meet the specific requirements of construction projects. It is clean, free of impurities, and often comes from natural sources like riverbeds or quarries. Building sand is commonly used for purposes such as making concrete, mortar, plaster, and for general masonry work in construction of the mosque.

AGGREGATE

Aggregate is a collective term for various small, rounded or crushed stones that are mixed with other construction materials like cement and sand to create concrete or other building materials. These pebbles or aggregates contribute to the strength and durability of the construction material.

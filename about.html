The ongoing construction of Omar Bin Khatwab mosque at Kwandomo, Malindi is a testament to our community's dedication and commitment to our faith. By contributing to this noble endeavor, you'll be making a meaningful and lasting impact, honoring our shared values and the spirit of unity at Kwandomo, Malindi
